Why It Matters
The Last-Mile Problem in Enterprise AI
Most enterprise AI deployments stop at the edge of the actual system. The AI advises. The human executes. This is the "last mile" — and it's where productivity gains evaporate. ExtraSuite closes the last mile. The agent doesn't just generate — it acts, inside the tool, under the company's governance policies.
Why Open Source
Trust Through Transparency
Enterprise IT teams don't deploy black-box AI to core workflows. Open source removes the trust barrier — security teams can audit the code, compliance teams can verify the governance model, and developers can extend it. This is the right architecture for an enterprise play.
