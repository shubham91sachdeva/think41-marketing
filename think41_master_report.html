Marketing Audit
SEVEN GAPS.ALL FIXABLE.

These aren't opinions. These are observable facts about what exists and what's missing. Click any gap to expand the full diagnosis and fix.

GAP 01
think41.com is invisible to Google
Critical
+
The Problem
The website is built as a Vite/React single-page application. Every page renders client-side in JavaScript. Search engine crawlers see a blank HTML shell with zero content. Zero pages indexed. Zero organic traffic. Zero inbound from search. This isn't a "we rank low" problem — it's a "we don't exist in Google" problem.
Verified: Google Search Console would show 0 indexed pages. No Think41 content appears for any branded or unbranded search queries about their products.
The Fix
Rebuild the site using a static site generator: Next.js (SSG mode), Gatsby, or Astro. This is a one-time engineering investment — not a recurring cost. Priority pages: Home, About, ExtraSuite product page, Foundation Voice product page, Mindlap landing page, Dev41/Autopods page, Blog index, Contact. Target: 50+ indexed pages within 90 days of rebuild. Engineering cost: ₹1.5L–₹3L one-time.

Content Strategy
4 PILLARS THATBUILD PIPELINE.

Not blog posts for blog's sake. Every piece of content should either rank in Google, build trust with a buyer, or activate a developer who becomes a community member. Anything that doesn't serve one of those three functions is a distraction.

🔧
Build in Public
How ExtraSuite was architected. Why Foundation Voice is open-source. What the team learned deploying GenAI at Fortune 500. Real decisions, real trade-offs, real failures. The content no competitor can replicate because they don't have Think41's actual experience.
  • "Why we chose service-account-per-user instead of a shared OAuth token pool"
  • "What we got wrong in our first 3 enterprise AI deployments"
  • "The architecture behind ExtraSuite's Google Workspace integration"
💡
AI Practitioner Takes
Sharp, original POV on LLMs, agentic systems, enterprise AI adoption, and the real gap between what's being promised and what actually works in production. Not news aggregation — actual opinions from people who are shipping.
  • "LLMs in enterprise: what the hype doesn't tell you about production"
  • "Why most enterprise AI projects fail after the proof of concept"
  • "What we're seeing in Fortune 500 GenAI adoption in 2026"
📊
Customer Outcomes
Case studies from real deployments. Specific outcomes: how many hours saved, what processes changed, what implementation looked like, what didn't work the first time. Even anonymised enterprise case studies build more trust than any marketing claim.
  • "How a Fortune 500 financial firm cut document processing time by 60%"
  • "What 6 months of AI-driven engineering delivery actually looked like"
  • "Voice AI in enterprise: what worked, what didn't, what surprised us"
🏗️

HashedIn → Deloitte acquisition → Deloitte MDs → Think41. Why leave a comfortable MD role to build again? What did Deloitte teach them about enterprise that they couldn't have learned anywhere else? This story has never been told publicly — and it's Think41's strongest trust signal.
